About $DEM

Demeter known as CERES

was one of the most prominent and powerful goddesses in all of ancient Greek mythology.
She is the goddess of the grains. She represents a cultivated earth, bountiful and Bountiful harvests, She blesses people with agricultural gifts through healthy crops and a good harvest each year.
Her goddess role extends throughout multiple legends and myths, from her origin to her offspring.
